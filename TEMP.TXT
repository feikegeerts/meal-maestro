  Technical Considerations for Green Screen

  Optimal Setup Prompt:
  Professional product photography setup with a tablet/smartphone positioned at 45-degree
  angle on a kitchen counter. Bright, even lighting eliminates shadows on the green screen.
  Device screen is perfectly flat and evenly lit green (#00FF00). Kitchen background has

  compositing.
